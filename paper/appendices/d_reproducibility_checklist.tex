\section{Reproducibility Checklist}
\label{app:reproducibility-checklist}

The draft follows checklist-style reporting discipline \cite{pineau2021reproducibility}:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
\item artifact-backed seed identities and raw-score hashes;
\item frozen manifests, support tuples, leakage counts, and output hashes;
\item exact normal-only threshold policy and metric definitions;
\item pair-grouped bootstrap uncertainty;
\item dedicated TempGlitch and R5-XGame validators;
\item a registered claim boundary and paragraph-level paper audit;
\item generated context for current status, handoff, and next action; and
\item release checks for tests, formatting, claims, context, secrets, and prohibited artifacts.
\end{itemize}

Remaining submission work includes official Springer-kit compilation, page-fit review, figure
production from approved artifacts, bibliography review, anonymization, and similarity screening.
