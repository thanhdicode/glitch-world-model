\section{Forbidden Claims for the Current Draft}
\label{app:forbidden-claims}

The manuscript must not introduce the following as positive claims:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
\item state-of-the-art performance or broad superiority;
\item universal or production-ready gameplay glitch detection;
\item direct TempGlitch--R5-XGame comparability or cross-game generalization;
\item binary-benchmark performance from R5-WOB;
\item SIGReg or action-conditioning benefit;
\item real-time capability;
\item temporal localization from binary video labels; or
\item locked-test performance.
\end{itemize}

Allowed result wording must remain frozen-split, non-locked, same-support, bounded, and explicit
about small support, uncertainty, and operating-point weakness.
