\section{Datasets and Evidence Scope}
\label{sec:datasets}

Table~\ref{tab:dataset-inventory} separates the paper's three evidence surfaces. TempGlitch is the
main public-benchmark lineage and spans five categories in the research MVP source. The
pair-disjoint follow-up uses only its non-locked validation roles. R5-XGame is a distinct,
positive-heavy binary family derived from the controlled World of Bugs expansion and is used only
as secondary bounded evidence.

R5-WOB is retained as a provenance-bound positive-probe result. Its evaluation set contains buggy
positives but no held normal-negative episodes, so it demonstrates pipeline execution and
class-conditional signal presence under a normal-calibrated threshold, not binary discrimination.
No WOB binary-benchmark metric is promoted into the paper.
