\section{Experimental Setup}
\label{sec:experiments}

The TempGlitch follow-up evaluates artifact-backed LeWM checkpoints from seeds 42, 43, and 44.
Six latent-surprise window scorers are combined with three episode aggregations, producing 54 LeWM
rows. The two baselines, \texttt{frame\_diff} and \texttt{feature\_distance}, are each combined
with the same three episode aggregations, producing six baseline rows. The headline comparison
selects the highest recorded AUROC row within each family for descriptive reporting; it does not
use evaluation results to retrain a model or refit a threshold.

R5-XGame contains the same 60-row configuration structure on its own frozen 72-episode evaluation
support. Its best recorded rows are presented as a separate secondary comparison. Descriptive
seed, scorer, and aggregation summaries from that bundle are exploratory; no causal SIGReg,
action-conditioning, temporal-smoothing, or training-budget ablation exists.

The repository also preserves window-level LeWM score series for qualitative inspection, but the
current validated artifacts do not expose true temporal event spans. Those timelines therefore
support illustrative figures only and are not evaluated with temporal localization metrics.

The earlier one-buggy-episode window pilot, old exposed sequential-subset results, and results from
different manifests are excluded from the main tables. Learned baselines that were not evaluated
on the exact frozen follow-up support are also excluded rather than treated as comparable.
