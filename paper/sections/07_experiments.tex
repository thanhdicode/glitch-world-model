\section{Bounded Experiments}
\label{sec:experiments}

The experiment section is currently restricted to the non-locked TempGlitch validation-only
identical-episode family documented in the repository's R5 results note. Required methods in this
family are LeWM latent surprise using artifact-backed seed42, seed43, and seed44 checkpoints,
along with \texttt{frame\_diff} and train-normal-fitted \texttt{feature\_distance}.

Two potentially relevant comparison families are deliberately not elevated here. The earlier
one-buggy-episode pilot is too limited to serve as the main paper table, and deferred methods such
as the Conv3D autoencoder or a frozen video-representation baseline are not included in the current
identical-manifest runner. This keeps the experiment section aligned with what is actually frozen
and hash-addressable today.

Every future addition to this section should first update the claim map and source matrix so the
paper remains evidence-led instead of convenience-led.
