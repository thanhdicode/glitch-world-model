\section{Limitations}
\label{sec:limitations}

The bounded results are non-locked and validation-only. The TempGlitch follow-up contains only 12
normal-negative and 22 buggy-positive evaluation episodes, and its operating threshold is
calibrated from only two normal episodes. Its best LeWM AUROC interval overlaps the best baseline
interval, and its FPR@95TPR remains 0.7500. The R5-XGame split is positive-heavy at 60 positives
and 12 negatives and exhibits seed sensitivity.

The two result families are not directly comparable because their support, calibration, domain,
action mode, and training lineage differ. R5-WOB remains a positive-probe family with no
normal-negative evaluation episodes. Binary video/episode labels do not establish temporal
localization. No controlled evidence supports SIGReg benefit, action-conditioning benefit, broad
cross-game generalization, or state of the art. Locked test remains unmaterialized and unscored.

The bibliography is still a bounded verified surface rather than an exhaustive review, and the
threshold operating points are validation-only rather than evidence of deployed calibration.
These limitations are part of the paper's integrity model and must remain visible in the main text.
