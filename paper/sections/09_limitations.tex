\section{Limitations}
\label{sec:limitations}

The present paper scaffold has at least five important limitations.

First, the bounded results are non-locked and validation-only. Second, the bibliography is still a
bounded verified surface rather than an exhaustive review. Third, the empirical family is narrow
enough that broad generalization claims would be unjustified even when a single row looks
favorable. Fourth, World of Bugs is not yet a paper-facing evaluation result, so any discussion of
WOB must remain at the status or workflow level. Fifth, window-level scores are diagnostic outputs
rather than validated temporal-localization evidence. Sixth, the current thresholding procedure is
a validation-only operating point, not evidence of deployed calibration.

These limitations are not incidental footnotes. They are part of the paper's integrity model and
should remain visible in the main text as later prose is added.
